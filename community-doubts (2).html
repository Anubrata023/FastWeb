📜 Community Rules
StudyVerse is a space built on respect, curiosity, and growth. To keep it safe and productive for everyone — especially during high-pressure exam season — please follow these rules.
01
Be Respectful Always
Treat every member with kindness. No bullying, mocking wrong answers, or belittling anyone's rank or score. We were all beginners once.
02
No Spam or Promotion
Don't post repetitive messages, advertisements, referral codes, or external coaching promotional content. Genuine resource sharing is welcome.
03
Post in Correct Channels
Use #doubts for academic questions, #jee-strategy for exam planning, #general for casual chat. Off-topic posts will be moved.
04
No Sharing Exam Cheats
Sharing exam paper leaks, unauthorised answer keys, or any content that facilitates cheating is strictly prohibited and will lead to an immediate ban.
05
Cite Your Sources
When sharing notes, solutions, or strategies from other creators, credit them. Academic integrity is the foundation of real learning.
06
Mental Health First
If someone is stressed or struggling, support them. Do not compare scores or rank others publicly. Encourage, don't discourage.
07
Keep It Academic
This is a study-focused space. Political debates, religious discussions, and off-topic controversial content are not permitted.
08
Mark Doubts as Solved
When your doubt is answered, mark it as solved so others can find clean, resolved threads. It helps the entire community.
⚠️ Violations
Breaking these rules may result in warnings, temporary muting, or permanent removal from the community. Moderators have the final say. Be the kind of student you'd want studying beside you.
